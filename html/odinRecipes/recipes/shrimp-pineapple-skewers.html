Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers

Description

Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers are a delightful blend of succulent shrimp and sweet pineapple, enhanced by a homemade teriyaki sauce. This sauce, featuring soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger, balances sweet, salty, and umami flavors, creating a unique, tasty marinade for the shrimp. Finished with sesame seeds and green onions, the dish boasts additional crunch and a vibrant color palette.

In this recipe, the shrimp, marinated in the teriyaki sauce and then grilled, is a flavor-packed star. The addition of grilled pineapple provides a sweet, slightly charred counterpoint to the savory shrimp.
